How to Survive

🎮 MOVE — WASD / Arrows. Touch joystick on mobile. Scroll to zoom.

🤚 INTERACT — Press E near plants, structures, NPCs. Harvest, craft, battle.

⚗️ CRAFT — TAB opens sidebar. Build structures, fuse creatures, manage world.

🌙 NIGHT — Sanity drains. Shadow creatures attack. Stay near campfire!

🌦 WEATHER — Rain boosts plants. Storms bring lightning. Spore winds spawn mushrooms.

📅 SEASONS — Spring→Summer→Autumn→Winter. Each changes growth, enemies, weather.

☠️ CORRUPTION — Toxic biome spreads each turn. Heal biomes to push it back!

🏗 BUILD — Place walls, gardens, lights. Structures persist in world.

🐾 COMPANIONS — Forged creatures follow you, auto-fight, auto-harvest nearby.

🌀 PRESTIGE — Win a victory, then Ascend for +15% permanent power.

💾 SAVE — Auto-saves. Press L to load.

🏆 4 WINS — Science 80% | Nature: all biomes 70%+ | War: 5 rivals | Exodus: 3 planets

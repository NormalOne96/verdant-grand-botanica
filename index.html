How to Survive

🎮 MOVEMENT — WASD or Arrow Keys to walk. Touch joystick on mobile. Your botanist explores the dying world in real time.

🤚 INTERACT — Press E near plants, mushrooms, structures. Harvest plants, use the Cauldron, access the Research Station, enter the Battle Arena.

⚗️ CRAFTING — Press TAB or tap ⚗️ to open the crafting sidebar. Build structures, craft items, fuse creatures.

🌿 225 SPECIES — Flowers, herbs, trees, vines, cacti, aquatics, mosses, and 75 rare mushrooms across 8 rarity tiers. Each has unique stats.

⚗️ FUSION — Combine 2-4 plants at the Cauldron to forge creatures. 150+ named recipes. Mushrooms multiply stats 1.5×-4×.

🌙 DAY/NIGHT — Sanity drops at night. Creatures grow hostile. Stay near your campfire or use bioluminescent plants.

🏗 BUILD — Place structures: Cauldron, Research Lab, Barracks, Observatory, and more. Each provides permanent bonuses.
